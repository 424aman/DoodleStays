Appliance instructions

Kitchen guide

Straightforward instructions for the home’s most important kitchen appliances.

Before you begin

A few helpful notes.

Important: Replace the sample instructions below with the exact model-specific steps for the appliances in your home. Include photos of buttons and control panels whenever possible.

Appliance 01

Refrigerator and ice maker

  1. Confirm the control panel is unlocked.
  2. Use the temperature buttons to adjust the refrigerator or freezer.
  3. Select cubed or crushed ice before pressing a glass against the dispenser.
  4. Do not hold the dispenser continuously for more than a few seconds.
Tip: The ice maker may pause if the freezer door has recently been open for an extended period.

Appliance 02

Oven and range

  1. Press the desired cooking mode, such as Bake or Convection Bake.
  2. Enter the temperature using the control panel.
  3. Press Start and allow the oven to preheat fully.
  4. Press Cancel when cooking is complete.
Safety: Never place foil directly on the bottom of the oven, and confirm all burners are off after use.

Appliance 03

Coffee maker

  1. Fill the water reservoir with fresh water.
  2. Insert the appropriate filter or coffee pod.
  3. Place a mug or carafe securely beneath the dispenser.
  4. Select the desired size and press Brew.
Tip: Empty used grounds or pods after each use and wipe away any spills.

Appliance 04

Dishwasher

  1. Scrape large food scraps from dishes.
  2. Load dishes without blocking the spray arms.
  3. Place one dishwasher pod in the detergent compartment.
  4. Select Normal and press Start before closing the door.
Please note: Use only dishwasher detergent. Regular dish soap can overflow and damage the machine.

Appliance 05

Microwave and specialty appliances

  1. Use microwave-safe cookware only.
  2. Enter the cooking time, then press Start.
  3. Use lower power settings for delicate reheating.
  4. Wipe the interior if food splatters.
