Local restaurants

Ocean City has plenty of casual dining options for families, groups, and nights out.

  • Thai O-Cha Thai dishes, curries, noodles, and flavorful appetizers.
  • Albertino’s Pizza Pizza and casual Italian food.
  • Blue Fish Sushi, seafood, and Asian-inspired dishes.
  • Liquid Assets A popular choice for dinner, drinks, and small plates.
  • The Original Greene Turtle Casual sports-bar food, and an Ocean City institution.

Jet ski and boat rentals

Spend an afternoon on the bay with a jet ski or boat rental.

Bayside Boat Rentals & Jet Ski Rentals

A convenient option for jet ski rentals and other ways to explore the bay.

  • Reserve ahead during busy summer weekends
  • Bring a valid photo ID
  • Follow all rental-company safety instructions

Ocean City Boardwalk

Walk the boardwalk, grab a snack, browse the shops, and enjoy the classic Ocean City atmosphere.

  • Thrasher’s French Fries A classic boardwalk snack best enjoyed with vinegar.
  • Fisher’s Popcorn Pick up a tub of Old Bay caramel popcorn to share.
  • Amusement Park on the Pier Rides, games, and family entertainment overlooking the ocean.
☀️

Relax at the beach

Pack towels, sunscreen, water, and a good book, then enjoy a relaxing day by the ocean.

  • Swim only when conditions are safe
  • Keep an eye on children near the water
  • Bring water and reapply sunscreen regularly
  • Rinse off sand before returning to the house
🐚

Beach walks and shelling

Early morning and evening are great times for a peaceful walk along the shoreline.

  • Look for shells, sea glass, and other beach treasures
  • Watch for horseshoe crabs near the shoreline
  • Leave living animals where you find them
  • Check the tide before taking a longer walk
🌅

Enjoy a quiet evening

Slow down after a busy day with dinner at home, a sunset walk, or a relaxing evening overlooking the water.

  • Take an evening walk on the beach
  • Watch the sunset over the bay
  • Play board games or watch a movie together
  • Observe neighborhood quiet hours

Appliance instructions

Kitchen guide

Straightforward instructions for the home’s most important kitchen appliances.

A few helpful notes.

Invisacook induction range

How to operate the range

Follow these instructions and be sure to use the invisacook mats as described in the tutorial below. Feel free to refer to this Invisacook Video Tutorial

6-step photo guide

Refrigerator

Refrigerator and ice maker

  1. Please find an additional ice maker behind the boat bar
  2. Select cubed or crushed ice before pressing a glass against the dispenser.
  3. Please close after use to avoid spoilage of fridge contents
Tip: The ice maker may pause if the freezer door has recently been open for an extended period.

Wolf steam oven

Oven and range

  1. Press the desired cooking mode, such as Bake or Convection Bake.
  2. Enter the temperature using the control panel.
  3. Press Start and allow the oven to preheat fully.
  4. Press Cancel when cooking is complete.
Safety: Never place foil directly on the bottom of the oven, and confirm all burners are off after use.

Cuisinart Coffee Center

Coffee maker

  1. Fill the correct water reservoir with fresh water(single K-cup or pot)
  2. Insert the appropriate filter or coffee pod.
  3. Place a mug or carafe securely beneath the dispenser.
  4. Select the desired size and press Brew.
Tip: Empty used grounds or pods after each use and wipe away any spills.
